                    <h2 class="sect-title-two fw-bold mb-20">
                      Why keyword analysis matters
                    </h2>
                    <p class="text-white mb-45">
                      User behavior is constantly evolving, and without current
                      data it’s easy to invest in topics with little or no demand.
                      Many websites still don’t name their pages according to what
                      people actually search for. As a result, content doesn’t
                      match real queries and campaigns target overly broad terms.
                      Keyword analysis identifies what is worth supporting and
                      where you are wasting resources.
                    </p>
                    <h2 class="sect-title-two fw-bold mb-20">
                      What the analysis delivers
                    </h2>
                    <p class="text-white mt-15 mb-45">
                      You will gain a clear overview of keywords and queries,
                      categorized by topic and priority. This shows you which
                      terms to target with content, how to structure your website,
                      and how to optimize PPC campaigns without wasting budget.
                      The result is a data-driven strategy that attracts more
                      relevant visitors while reducing promotion costs.
                    </p>
                    <h2 class="sect-title-two fw-bold mb-20">
                      How the process works
                    </h2>
                    <p class="text-white">
                      The process starts with collecting data from Google and
                      Seznam search engines, combined with outputs from Google
                      Analytics and Search Console. Next comes cleaning, sorting,
                      and clustering keywords by relevance, search volume, and
                      your site’s ranking positions. The deliverable is a
                      structured table of keywords, content recommendations, and
                      PPC insights. The analysis always reflects STDC
                      (See-Think-Do-Care) stages, so you know how to cover the
                      entire buying journey.
                    </p>
