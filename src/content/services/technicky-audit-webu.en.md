                    <p class="text-white mb-35">

                      technical aspects of your website. It goes deeper than a

                      significantly hinder indexation, site speed, or user

                      technically prepare your website for maximum performance.

                    <h2 class="sect-title-two mb-15">
                      Why a technical audit now
                    </h2>
                    <p class="text-white mb-45">
                      Every website grows over time, while technologies and search
                      engine requirements continue to evolve. Google increasingly
                      emphasizes speed, Core Web Vitals, and proper content
                      accessibility. Even minor technical errors can cause ranking
                      drops or inefficient crawl budget usage. Without regular
                      technical audits, you risk losing traffic and facing
                      unnecessarily high maintenance costs.
                    </p>
                    <h2 class="sect-title-two">What the audit delivers</h2>
                    <p class="text-white mt-15 mb-45">
                      The technical SEO audit provides an in-depth view of your
                      site’s condition – from indexation, sitemaps, and robots.txt
                      to JavaScript rendering, loading speed, and security
                      settings. You receive a clear overview of issues, their
                      impact on performance, and recommendations for resolving
                      them. The audit serves as a practical foundation for both
                      developers and marketers, ensuring technical improvements
                      deliver measurable business results.
                    </p>
                    <h2 class="sect-title-two fw-bold mb-20">
                      How the process works
                    </h2>
                    <p class="text-white">
                      The process begins with data collection from Google Search
                      Console, Analytics, and crawling tools. This is followed by
                      a thorough review of indexation and content accessibility,
                      analysis of speed and Core Web Vitals, and an evaluation of
                      JavaScript rendering. The audit also includes checks of
                      redirects, canonical URLs, and structured data. The
                      deliverable is a technical report with detailed issue
                      descriptions and a prioritized roadmap of fixes.
                    </p>
