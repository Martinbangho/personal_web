                    <h2 class="sect-title-two fw-bold mb-20">
                      Why address an audit now
                    </h2>
                    <p class="text-white mb-45">
                      Search engines operate differently than they did just a few
                      years ago. New algorithms, AI Overviews, and changing user
                      behavior mean that without regular audits, you risk losing
                      visibility. The longer you postpone an audit, the more
                      expensive and complex recovery becomes. Studies show that
                      more than two-thirds of websites contain critical technical
                      issues that limit their performance.
                    </p>
                    <h2 class="sect-title-two fw-bold mb-20">
                      What the SEO audit delivers
                    </h2>
                    <p class="text-white mt-15 mb-45">
                      The audit provides a clear view of how your site performs
                      for both search engines and users. You’ll receive
                      recommendations that remove technical barriers, improve
                      speed and indexation, and highlight where to strengthen
                      content or backlinks. The analysis also includes competitor
                      benchmarking, so you know exactly where growth opportunities
                      lie and how to seize them.
                    </p>
                    <h3 class="sect-title-two fw-bold mb-20">
                      How the SEO audit works
                    </h3>
                    <p class="text-white">
                      The process starts with an initial consultation to align on
                      goals and expectations. This is followed by an analysis of
                      traffic and technical health, a review of content, metadata,
                      and site structure. Next, I assess the backlink profile, its
                      quality and risks, and compare your performance with key
                      competitors. The outcome is a strategic document – a
                      prioritized roadmap with clear recommendations based on
                      impact and complexity.
                    </p>

                    <p class="text-white pb-5">
