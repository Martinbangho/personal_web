                      identity elements, tone of voice, and recommendations for
                      managing marketing teams. The result is a document that
                      eliminates chaos and defines clear priorities.

                      available data. Personas, segmentation, and channel
                      proposals are then created. The final deliverable is a
                      presentation of results and onboarding of your internal team
                      to ensure the strategy is effectively implemented and
                      evaluated.
